\vec{u} =u_1\mathbf{i}+u_2\mathbf{j}+u_3\mathbf{k}

$$
\mathbf{u}\times \mathbf{v}=(u_2v_3-u_3v_2)\mathbf{i}-(u_1v_3-u_3v_1)\mathbf{j}+(u_1v_2-u_2v_1)\mathbf{k}
