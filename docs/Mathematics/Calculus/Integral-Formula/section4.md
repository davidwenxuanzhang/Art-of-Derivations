# Integral with $ax^2+b$

### 22
$$
\int \frac{1}{ax^2+b} \ \mathrm{d}x = 
\left\{\begin{aligned}
&\frac{1}{\sqrt{ab}}\arctan\sqrt{\frac{a}{b}}x+C \left(b>0\right)\\
&\frac{1}{2\sqrt{-ab}}\ln\left|\frac{\sqrt{a}x-\sqrt{-b}}{\sqrt{a}x+\sqrt{-b}}\right|+C\left(b<0\right)
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$
